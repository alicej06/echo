Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

MAIA ASL ("we", "our", or "the app") is built by MAIA Biotech. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

Data We Collect

MAIA ASL collects no personal data. Specifically:

  • EMG signals are processed entirely on your device or sent to our inference server for real-time classification. They are not stored, logged, or associated with any user identity.
  • Calibration data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. It never leaves your phone.
  • Settings (server URL, device name, speech rate) are stored locally on your device. They are never transmitted.

No Account Required

MAIA ASL does not require an account, email address, or any personal information to use.

Bluetooth

The app requests Bluetooth permission solely to connect to the Myo EMG wristband. Bluetooth data is used only for real-time EMG signal streaming and is not stored or transmitted beyond the inference pipeline.

Inference Server

When using the Railway WebSocket inference server, raw EMG window data (40 samples x 8 channels) is transmitted over an encrypted WebSocket connection for classification. This data is not stored on the server and is discarded immediately after the prediction is returned.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking SDKs.

Children

MAIA ASL is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
